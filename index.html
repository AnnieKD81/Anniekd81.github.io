About me

Immigrant. Coder. Traveler. Teacher. Journalist. I have taken many paths in my life, and continue to meander on my journey, but one thing is constant - writing. Film and TV writing continue to be my preferred methods of expression. Doing what I love as a career is a perk and a priviledge.

I am drawn to stories about smart women whose rebellion is necessary for their survivial. Whether their way of fighting back is physical, psychological or emotional, they take measures into their own hands to carve out a life on their own terms. I also love stories about non-traditional men that allow them to be full humans, instead of macho caricatures.

One of the things that led me to writing is my diverse range of interests. I am tri-lingual (English, Polish, French), built this website after hacking a template, read like a fiend and research like a pro, have lived abroad, have Polish and US Citizenship, throw knives for fun, spent my college years skateboarding, am obsessed with the Tour de France, and well, there's too much to list. But I have spent my life doing, seeing, tasting, and talking, and I did it because it was what I wanted to do, but I bring that experience into my work.
